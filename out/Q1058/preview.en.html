नज जवन

Narendra Modi was born on 17 September 1950 in a middle-class family of Heeraben Modi and Damodardas Moolchand Modi in Vadnagar village in Mahesana district of the erstwhile Bombay state.[1](from hiwiki: नरेन्द्र मोदी) He was the third of six children born.[1](from hiwiki: नरेन्द्र मोदी) Modi's family belonged to the 'Modh-Ghanchi-Teli' community, which is classified as Other Backward Classes by the Government of India.[1](from hiwiki: नरेन्द्र मोदी)
